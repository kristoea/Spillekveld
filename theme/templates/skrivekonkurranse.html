Think you've got what it takes to design a one-shot adventure? Then sharpen your quills and ready your dice, because the Midgard Con Module Writing Competition is officially open.

This is your chance to show off your storytelling skills and have your work celebrated by fellow TTRPG enthusiasts.

Midgard Con has teamed up with Outland, and we are offering A 1,000kr Outland Voucher for First place and a 750kr Outland voucher for second place.



The Submission Rules Deadline:

  1. All entries must be received by 22.08.2025 (that's August 22nd).
  2. Eligibility: You must have a valid ticket to Midgard Con 2025 to enter.
  3. One Entry Per Person: This is Midguard Con... Original work only: Modules must be your own creation. No plagiarising, re-skins, or AI-generated evil (we will be doing our due diligence).
  4. No Repeats: Modules submitted or published at previous conventions will be disqualified (yes, we will check).
  5. Time Limit: Your module must be playable and finishable in three hours or less. Word Limit: Maximum 3,500 words.


Submission Requirements Format:

  1. Submit as a PDF or Word document. No scanned pages or photos of your dog's chewing accident, please.
  2. Language: Modules must be written in Norwegian or English.
  3. Content Warnings: Please include appropriate warnings if your adventure contains sensitive content (e.g., horror, violence).
  4. System Requirements: Modules should be system-neutral or clearly specify the system used (e.g. Dragepust, D&D 5e, Mork Borg, etc.).


Judging Criteria:

Entries will be judged on creativity, clarity, playability, and suitability for a 3-hour one-shot.

Rights & Usage: By entering, you grant Midgard Con permission to share excerpts of your module for promotional purposes. You retain full copyright.



How to Submit:

Email your entry to Info@Jamiemandrews.com with the subject line: "Midgard Con Module Contest Entry - [Your Name]"
